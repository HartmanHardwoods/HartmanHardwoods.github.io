We're a family-owned farm located in the small town of Scottsville, KY.

Our main goal is to help educate families and communities about good gardening strategies, ecosystem management, and the cultivation of native woodland species for a healthy and diverse food supply.

We have and will continue to contribute to research and development around technologies and unique, diverse plant material for the benefit of humanity and wildlife resources.
